Welcome to the Land of the Perfect Cone

Located in the southeastern tip of Luzon in the Bicol Region, Albay is a province defined by its resilient history and the iconic Mayon Volcano. Whether you're seeking adventure or cultural immersion, Albay offers an unforgettable experience.

Adventure

Ride ATVs over lava trails or hike the slopes of Lignon Hill.

Sightseeing

From Cagsawa Ruins to Sumlang Lake, every corner is picture-perfect.

Cuisine
