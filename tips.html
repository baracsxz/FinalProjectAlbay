Travel Tips

Important safety and financial reminders for a hassle-free stay.

Always check for volcano activity updates from PHIVOLCS before hiking. Stay hydrated and keep your bags secure in crowded areas.

Albay food is famous for being spicy. If you cannot tolerate heat, ask the cook to prepare your meal with mild spice levels.

Most small shops and local drivers only accept cash. It is best to withdraw money at an ATM in the city center before exploring.

Taking many photos and videos will drain your battery quickly. A portable charger will keep your phone powered all day long.

Mobile signals can be weak in mountainous areas. Download an offline map of Albay on your phone before you start your journey.

Travel Guide

How to get around and what to expect during your visit.

1. Best Time to Visit

Plan your trip from March to May for dry weather and the clearest views of Mayon Volcano.

2. Transportation

Use jeepneys for town-to-town travel and tricycles for short distances inside the city centers.

3. Arrival Points

You can fly into Bicol International Airport or take a provincial bus from Manila to Legazpi City.

4. What to Pack

Bring light cotton clothes, comfortable walking shoes, and an umbrella for sudden rain showers.

5. Local Etiquette

Be polite to locals and dress modestly when entering old churches or historical holy sites.
